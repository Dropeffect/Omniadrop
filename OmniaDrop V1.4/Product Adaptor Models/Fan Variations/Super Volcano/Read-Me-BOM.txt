You need the following additional materials:

1x 4010 Blower Fan
2x ISO4762 M2x4mm for the top holes of the 4010 blower fans
2x ISO4762 M2x6mm for the bottom holes of the 4010 blower fans
2x ISO7380 M3x8mm to attach the "Cooling-Two-Fan" to the "Cooling-Block-Fan"
4x M3 threaded inserts, these are there to attach a probe holder. The top two threaded insert are intended for a BLTouch probe holder and the the bottom two threaded inserts are intended for a induction probe holder.

For reference there are two pictures how to assemble everything.
